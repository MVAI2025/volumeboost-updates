VolumeBoost Audio is live on the Mac App Store

VolumeBoost Audio is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that makes quiet audio louder across apps, browsers, videos, speakers, AirPods, headphones, and other connected audio devices.

For most users, the Mac App Store version is the recommended install. Updates are delivered through Apple.
