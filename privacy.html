VolumeBoost

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

VolumeBoost is a macOS menu bar utility that makes quiet audio louder on your Mac. Audio processing happens locally on your device.

Audio

VolumeBoost may request macOS audio recording access so it can process system audio locally. VolumeBoost does not upload, sell, or share your audio. Audio is not stored by the app.

Diagnostics

VolumeBoost can create a diagnostics file if you choose to export one. Diagnostics are saved locally and are only shared if you decide to send them for support.

Updates

The App Store version is updated through Apple. The direct-download version may check the VolumeBoost update feed to see whether a newer version is available.

Analytics and Tracking

VolumeBoost does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
